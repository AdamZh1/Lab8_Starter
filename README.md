Graceful degradation and service workers are related because service workers act as a form of graceful degradation. In this case, the network acts as a max technology, and service workers act as a lower level, in case the network fails. If the network were to fail, then instead of the whole application failing because it can't retrieve recipes, service workers can act without internet to retrieve recipes from the local cache, acting as a safeguard.
